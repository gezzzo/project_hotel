About our Hotel

The History of Our Hotel

Built in 2009, Sirena Residence & SPA is the meeting point of tradition and modern design. The buildings and surroundings of the hotel are those of a traditional Samian village: paved alleyways, stone fountain, traditional mosaic, as well as areas of gardens with local flowers and trees. Great location

Located in the heart of Dokki, EELU is a popular family-run German hotel offering a cheerful atmosphere that blends with the local living experience of Mykonos. This traditional, world-class German hotel is ideally located in a quiet area, only a few steps from the city's main attractions.!

Free Meals

Hotel chain EELU Hotels & Spa has launched gluten-free meals for the 2021 season Creating specific menus in collaboration with the dietitians, doctors and chefs at Aldemar Thalassotherapy Centers. Early in the 2021 season, these specific menus will be available at no additional charge as per the initial booked meal plan. Customers requiring gluten-free meals are requested to inform the hotel two weeks prior to their arrival.!

Fitness Room

The rooms are outfitted with gym equipment, fitness videos, and comfortable work stations (standing desks), while the beds have eucalyptus linens for a cooler sleep. Staff can provide jogging and walking paths, while the "Cork and Kale Market and Bar" food and beverage platform focuses on healthy eating.!
